About Me

My name is Nicholas Looney. You can call me Nick! I have over 15 years of experience managing projects, people, software, and training.

I’ve delivered valuable software solutions while working for large, high-tech companies like Micron, IM Flash, and Intel. I’ve also managed applications and training for a prestigious multi-office Nevada law firm.

But I left the corporate world to move back to my home state of Missouri and make my own way here.

My wife and I grew up in Cuba, MO. We fell in love while working together at the local Pizza Hut and have been married for over 15 years! ♡

We used to live a fairly unhealthy lifestyle but have lost over 100 pounds each together. Nowadays, we enjoy nutrition, hiking, becoming more self-reliant, and helping others through our web-based businesses.
